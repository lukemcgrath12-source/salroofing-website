Our Story

Quality, Reliability, and the Extra Mile

SAL Roofing was founded on a simple principle: deliver honest, high-quality work at fair prices. Sam Longmuir and the team have spent nearly a decade building a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and genuine care for every customer.

We specialise in residential roofing across Sydney and the South Coast, with deep expertise in coastal conditions, marine-grade materials, and the unique challenges that come with our climate. Whether it's a metal roof installation, an emergency repair, or routine maintenance, we approach every job with the same level of attention and professionalism.

What sets us apart is simple: we listen, we deliver, and we're willing to go the extra mile. We believe in repair-first solutions and won't recommend a replacement unless it's truly necessary. You can trust that we're looking out for your best interests, not our profit margin.
