Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

SAL Roofing ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the Australian Privacy Principles set out in the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

1. Information we collect

We collect information you give us directly when you request a quote, book an inspection, or contact us. This may include:

  • Your name, email address and phone number
  • Your property or job-site address
  • Details about the roofing work you're enquiring about

We also collect limited technical information automatically when you visit our website, such as your device type, browser, and pages viewed, through analytics tools (see section 5).

2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide quotes
  • Schedule inspections and carry out roofing work
  • Communicate with you about your job, including follow-ups
  • Handle insurance documentation where relevant
  • Improve our website and services

3. Disclosure of your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it only where necessary — for example with suppliers, insurers, or contractors directly involved in completing your job, or where required by law.

4. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from misuse, loss, and unauthorised access. Enquiries submitted through our website are processed via secure third-party services. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

5. Cookies and analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This may involve cookies that collect anonymous usage data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. If we run advertising through Google or Meta, those platforms may also use cookies to measure ad performance.

6. Your rights

You can ask to access or correct the personal information we hold about you, or request that we delete it, by contacting us using the details below. We'll respond within a reasonable timeframe.
